About Us

Make Memories with River Expeditions

Book Now

River Expeditions was founded by a pair of entrepreneurs, who had a passion for white water rafting. The business was started in 1974 and has steadily grown and now offers rafting tours on many of the wild untamed rivers in the surrounding states. We have the latest safety equipment aboard each of our rafts and each rafting group is outfitted with a satellite phone for use in emergency situations. We offer overnight camping on our multiday trips which can include meals with excellent cuisine. Our tour guides have many hours of experience in each of our rivers. We invite you to contact us via telephone, email or social media to learn more about our tours and amenities. We feel certain that you will enjoy your tour and come back to join us… again and again. We invite you to share your experiences on our social media site and welcome any and all feedback. We look forward to seeing you soon! Happy Rafting!
