Overview

Purpose

The River Expeditions Website should present our business, capabilities and facilities to the public as a method to gain build market share. The Website should provide information about our established tours, costs, payment methods and tour packages. This will enable Website visitors to choose a tour(s) that fit their budget. The Website should provide information concerning rafting safety, potential risk and outline safety equipment and protocols. This will ensure the Website visitor is aware of the risks and our safety measures. The Website should provide links to river maps, equipment requirements (i.e., what to wear) and tour difficulties. This will inform Website visitors of our wide range of tour packages with their varying degrees of difficulty. The Website should provide links to upcoming events and tools for trip planning. This will assist the Website visitor to “plan ahead” and invite physical visits. The Website should share our physical address, mailing address, telephone number(s), email address(s) and business contact information. This will invite potential customers to make contact with our knowledgeable associates. The Website should contain a sitemap, a navigation bar, a header with links to services and a footer with links to email and social media sites such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and YouTube. This will invite repeat visits to the Website and provide potential advertisement through customer experience. Additional links for Website visitors to share comments, reviews and tour photographs should be provided. This will inform Website visitors of our excellent facilities, location and staff.

Audience

The River Expeditions Website should target middle to high income individuals, families and groups which enjoy the outdoors and enjoy physical challenges. The Website should share photos of families and individuals at various locations on the tours. Individuals, families and groups looking for excitement and challenge will be motivated to participate which will invite them to join a white-water rafting tour. The Website should target individuals, families and groups starting with a minimum age of 12, which are in good health and able to withstand the rigors of river travel. This will inform potential customers that we are family oriented and family friendly. The Website should target Corporate and Business team building groups, High adventure groups such as Boy Scout/Explorer/Adventure Scouting, Hiking and Adventure groups such as the Sierra Club. The Website should include keywords such as white-water rafting”, “adventure”, etc. to facilitate web searches which will ensure Website exposure. The Website should target people visiting websites for Sporting Goods stores and outdoor equipment retailers. The Website should be available for individuals and families looking for travel options and should target travel agencies, tour group organizers and providers of Destination Vacations. The Website should be accessible to all web connected devices such as mobile phones, tablets, desktop/laptop computers.
